Leadership

Professional Development

Build leadership skills and enhance leadership knowledge with targeted courses, certificate or degree programs.

Q Why take AU's Leadership courses / certificate / diploma?
A In today's increasingly complex organizations, leaders must create environments in which positive things happen, and inspired others to contribute at their highest levels. Leaders not only serve as change agents who guide, transform, and bring out the best in their employees to achieve extraordinary results...Read More
Q What is the time commitment?
A Leadership courses are 4 weeks in length and learners commit approximately 8-12 hours per week. The Leadership certificate of completion can be completed in as little as 1 year and the diploma in 2 years, students have up to 5 years to complete the diploma.

Designed for

Professionals seeking to develop leadership skills and deepen their knowledge of leadership fundamentals.

Develop a greater awareness of different leadership styles and preferences

Identify leadership blind spots and developmental opportunities

Understand your current leadership strengths and areas of challend through diagnostic tools for self evaluation

Challenge your leadership assumptions and develop new leadership skills and insights

Develop a greater awareness of why organizations change and the roles of leaders and managers within the process

Practice essential elements of coaching skills and coaching process with colleagues

Use a best practices model to plan for a change

Demonstrate increased awareness in and sensitivity to cross-cultural issues in negotiations
